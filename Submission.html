公開徵稿


從3/7起到3/30期間，Calice將要為未來推出的新模式與章節進行全球的樂曲的徵稿活動！
只要是你個人的原創作品且擁有完整版權（或是已被授權）都可以投稿。
Submission Email: Ra8bitsubmission@gmail.com
那以下是我們的投稿格式以及條件：
-
1.暫不收錄變速歌曲。
2.暫不收錄BPM > 220 or < 90的歌曲。
3.若您提供為複拍號之樂曲，收錄後需提供樂曲訊息跟midi協助譜師製作。
4.不收Remix/Bootleg等未授權之再製歌曲。
5.投稿請提供Wav. Mp3.樂曲之取樣率格式為44.1 Hz, 24bit。
6.每個人將可以提供最多3首歌曲。
7.請將投稿資料整理於能夠下載的google雲端資料夾。
8.請提供個樂曲：曲名、作者、BPM曲速。
9.樂曲長度介於1:50~2:20之間。

Song Submission


From 3/7 to 3/30, Calice is going to launch a global call for submissions for the new modes and chapters to be released in the future!
You can submit your own original composition (even if licensed), as long as it is fully copyrighted.
Submission Email: Ra8bitsubmission@gmail.com
Here are our submission formats and conditions.
-
1. No variable speed songs will be included at this time.
2. No songs with BPM > 220 or < 90.
3. Multi-time signature music is allowed, but you will be required to provide music information and midi file to assist the chart designers in production.
4. No Remix/Bootleg or other unlicensed remixes will be accepted.
5. Please provide Wav. Mp3 with the sample rate format of 44.1 Hz 24bit.
6. Each person can submit up to 3 songs.
7. Please organize your submissions in a google drive folder.
8. Please provide a song: title, artist and BPM.
9. The song should be between 1:50 and 2:20 in length.
